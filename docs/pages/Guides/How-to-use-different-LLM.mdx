# Setting Up Language Models for Your App

Setting up different language models for your app can enhance its capabilities by allowing it to understand and generate text using multiple engines. Depending on your needs, you can choose from cloud-based models or integrate local open-source LLMs.

## Available LLM Options

DocsGPT now supports a wide range of LLM providers. The available models include:

- OpenAI
- Azure OpenAI
- Sagemaker
- HuggingFace
- Llama.cpp
- Anthropic
- DocsGPT (via DocsGPTAPILLM)
- PremAI
- Groq
- Google
- Novita

## For Cloud-Based LLMs

<Steps>
### Step 1
Visit the chat screen to view the default LLM selected.
### Step 2
Click on the LLM selector to see a dropdown of available cloud-based LLM options.
### Step 3
Select the LLM of your choice.
</Steps>
